Description of Tiger

The tiger is the largest species of cat, known for its orange coat with black stripes.
Tigers are powerful hunters and symbolize strength and courage.
They are found mainly in Asia, inhabiting forests and grasslands.
Tigers are solitary animals and excellent swimmers.

Description of Lion

The lion is often called the "King of the Jungle," recognized by its majestic mane.
Lions live in groups called prides, showing strong social behavior.
They are native to Africa and parts of India.
Lions symbolize bravery, leadership, and royalty.

Description of Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals, known for their trunk and tusks.
They are highly intelligent and social, living in herds led by matriarchs.
Elephants play a vital role in their ecosystems by shaping habitats.
They symbolize wisdom, memory, and strength.
